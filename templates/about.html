About Arctos

Arctos, latin for bear, is a recursive backronym that stands for Arctos: Reid's Comprehensive Tournament Organization System.

It is a tool designed by TOs, for TOs, with the goal of reducing the tournament organization workload. The design philosophy has three main components:

  • capable & accessible: give all juggers easy access to the best available tools and data.
  • unopinionated: don't impose unnecessary structure onto how tournaments are organized.
  • minimal: collect all data necessary for functionality and nothing more.
Read more in the User Documentation.

Functionality

Flexible Scheduling

Give teams clear, predictable, and efficient schedules. Implement any tournament structure.

Live Updates

Get live score updates for matches as they happen, and overlay a scoreboard on your live stream in OBS.

Player Tracking

Log injuries and view results for all tournaments.

Registration

Manage team and playyer registrations and payment. Stripe payment portal coming soon.

Head Ref Tools

Automatically track point outcomes, scores, and stone count. Log notes on teams, players, and points.

YouTube Live Integration

Automatically scrub to points in live streams for easy footage analysis.
