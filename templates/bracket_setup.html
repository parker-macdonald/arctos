As it turns out, creating software to visualize brackets for arbitrary match graphs is very difficult. Restricting tournaments to only specific types of brackets is not an option because Arctos is designed to be rather unopinionated with regards to tournament structure. Instead, TOs are expected to create their own bracket, which can be parameterized using the form below.

Brackets are simply images with team labels placed on top. Upload an image of the bracket for your tournament with none of the teams filled out. Then, add team annotations in the same way as in the match setup page. Teams may be entered as explicit usernames, tags, or match results (match_name::winner, etc).

When a match finishes, the results will automatically be displayed in the bracket.
